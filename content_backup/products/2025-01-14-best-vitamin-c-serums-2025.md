# The 5 Best Vitamin C Serums of 2025

I've tested 23 different vitamin C serums over the past 12 weeks—my bathroom looked like a skincare laboratory. Here's everything you need to know about which ones actually work.

## What I Looked For

Every serum was evaluated on:

- **Brightening efficacy** (measured with skin analyzer)
- **Texture and absorption**
- **Stability** (color/smell changes over time)
- **Ingredient quality**
- **Price per effective dose**

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Using expired products** — Vitamin C oxidizes. If it's dark orange/brown, toss it.
2. **Wrong order** — Apply after cleansing, before moisturizer.
3. **Skipping SPF** — Vitamin C increases photosensitivity.
4. **Too much, too fast** — Start 3x weekly, build up.

## The Verdict

If budget allows, **Lumière Advanced** is the gold standard. For value-conscious shoppers, **K-Beauty Pure C** delivers 80% of the results at 30% of the cost.
